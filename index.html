NewCoding

NewCoding teaches students software development fundamentals, empowering them to use the abundance of free and low-cost learning resources available for professional developers.

NewCoding is a free program of the NewCo Foundation.

Hacker Summer Camp

A free, blended in-person and online course that introduces students aged 12-20 to full-stack web development.

Workshops are taught in-person by professional software developers on Wednesday nights at Rancho Campana High School in Camarillo, CA. Volunteer mentors float around the room assisting students one-on-one.

JavaScript is the core language of our stack. It offers a highly-portable, all-purpose ecosystem and is well-suited for web development.

Instructors & Mentors

Join Our Team

NewCoding is powered by a core team of volunteer instructors and mentors. We're always looking for tech professionals willing to make at least one 3-hour commitment each quarter on a Wednesnday night from 6-9p.

Instructors

Individual instructors teach our curriculum workshops. Workshops typically consist of code-along instruction followed by independent student exercises.

Mentors

Each workshop, several mentors float around the classroom, assisting students one-on-one. This includes answering questions, debugging, and teaching the tooling.
